there are 4 pricing model in aws

1. pay as you go: pay according to your usage
2. save when you reserved: offer a discount/cost saving plan if you plan to reserve resource for a long time
3. pay less by using more: discout offer if you use a service with high volume
4. pay less as AWS grow: discount from aws when aws grow. some discount may be offered to loyal customer
5. free service & free tier: a full free usage of a service like (IAM, VPC, cloud formation, beanstalk, auto scalling group, security group)
