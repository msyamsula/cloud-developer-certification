kinesis is a managed service that process a real-time big data

there is a sub service in amazon kinesis which are
1. kinesis stream, this is where your data come into contact with kinesis
2. kinesis analytic, real time analytic of the data stream that you get from kinesis stream
3. kinesis firehouse, a way to store your streaming data into S3, redshift, postgres, dynamoDB, documentDB etc.
4. kinesis video stream, stream a video data.
